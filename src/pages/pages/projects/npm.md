## webpack-sentry-plugin

[webpack-sentry-plugin](https://www.npmjs.com/package/webpack-sentry-plugin) is a webpack plugin to upload sourcemaps and built assets to [Sentry](https://sentry.io). I maintain the codebase and have contributed a handful of features. [Open source](https://github.com/40thieves/webpack-sentry-plugin).

## sitemap-webpack-plugin

[sitemap-webpack-plugin](https://www.npmjs.com/package/sitemap-webpack-plugin) is a webpack plugin to generate a sitemap for static sites. [Open source](https://github.com/schneidmaster/sitemap-webpack-plugin).

## react-bootstrap-maskedinput

[react-bootstrap-maskedinput](https://www.npmjs.com/package/react-bootstrap-maskedinput) is a [react-maskedinput](http://insin.github.io/react-maskedinput) form element that works with [react-bootstrap](https://react-bootstrap.github.io). [Open source](https://github.com/schneidmaster/react-bootstrap-maskedinput).
